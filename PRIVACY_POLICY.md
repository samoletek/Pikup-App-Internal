# Privacy Policy for PikUp

**Last Updated:** February 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction
PikUp ("PikUp," "we," "our," or "us") provides a platform that connects customers with independent drivers for item pickup and delivery. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and what choices you have.

By using the PikUp mobile application and related services (the "Service"), you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
- **Account and profile information:** name, email address, phone number, account role (customer or driver), and profile photo.
- **Authentication information:** login data for email/password and social sign-in (Google or Apple).
- **Trip and order details:** pickup and dropoff addresses, item descriptions, selected vehicle type, pricing inputs, and order status updates.
- **User content:** in-app messages, delivery notes, support/claim details, and uploaded photos or documents (for example, pickup/dropoff verification images or claim attachments).
- **Payment-related information:** payment method details are handled through Stripe; we do not store full card numbers or CVC codes in PikUp systems.
- **Driver onboarding information:** information submitted during driver onboarding and identity verification (including data processed through Stripe Identity), plus vehicle information.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
- **Location information:** precise location used for map features, trip routing, matching, and live trip updates. For drivers, location may be collected while actively using delivery/navigation features, including foreground use and (where enabled by device permissions) background updates.
- **Device and app data:** device type, operating system, app version, crash/performance diagnostics, and related technical logs.
- **Notification data:** push notification permissions and push token (if enabled).
- **Local app storage data:** cached session and app preference data stored on your device (for example, login/session persistence, selected preferences, and recent app state).

## 3. How We Use Information
We use information to:
- Provide and operate the Service.
- Create and manage accounts, authenticate users, and maintain security.
- Process and support trip requests, real-time tracking, payments, and communication between users.
- Verify identity and help prevent fraud, abuse, or unsafe activity.
- Support insurance/claim flows when selected by the user.
- Improve app reliability, features, and user experience.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

## 4. How We Share Information
We may share information with:
- **Other users in the Service:** for example, customers and drivers receive limited profile, trip, and location details needed to complete deliveries.
- **Service providers and infrastructure partners:** including Supabase (backend/auth/database/storage), Stripe and Stripe Identity (payments and verification), Mapbox (maps, geocoding, routing), Twilio (phone OTP verification), and other vendors required to operate the Service.
- **Insurance/claims providers or partner systems:** when needed to process optional coverage or claims requested through the Service.
- **Legal and safety authorities:** when required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
- **Business transfer scenarios:** if PikUp is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal information.

## 5. Data Retention
We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type (for example, account records, transaction records, trip history, or claims-related data).

## 6. Your Choices and Rights
- **Account deletion:** You can request deletion from within the app (Profile -> Personal Information -> Delete Account). Deletion is permanent and will remove account data as described by our systems and legal requirements.
- **Profile updates:** You can edit certain profile details in-app.
- **Location controls:** You can change location permissions in your device settings. Some features may not function without location access.
- **Notifications:** You can disable push notifications in your device settings.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information. You can submit requests to the contact email below.

## 7. Data Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy
The Service is intended only for users age 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version with a new "Last Updated" date. Material changes may also be communicated in-app or by email.
