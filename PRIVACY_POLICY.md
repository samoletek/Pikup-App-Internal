# Privacy Policy for PikUp

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to PikUp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and services.

**By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

## 2. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Account Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and password (managed via Supabase/Google/Apple Auth).
*   **Profile Data:** Profile pictures and rating information.
*   **Payment Information:** Credit card details and billing information (processed securely by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers).
*   **Driver Documentation:** Driver’s license, vehicle information, and insurance details (for Driver partners).
*   **Content:** Photos of items for pickup, delivery confirmation photos, and in-app messages.

### B. Information We Collect Automatically
*   **Location Data (Crucial):**
    *   **Customers:** We collect your precise location to facilitate pickup and delivery requests.
    *   **Drivers:** We collect precise location data in the **foreground and background** to match you with nearby requests, track deliveries in real-time, and calculate driver earnings. **Background location tracking is essential for the Driver experience** to ensure customers can track their delivery even when the Driver's app is minimized.
*   **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
*   **Usage Data:** Interaction with the app, timestamps, and error logs.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following purposes:
*   **Service Delivery:** To connect Customers with Drivers and facilitate the transport of items.
*   **Navigation & Tracking:** To utilize Mapbox services for routing and real-time delivery tracking.
*   **Payments:** To process secure transactions via Stripe.
*   **Safety & Security:** To verify user identity, monitor for fraud, and ensure the safety of deliveries.
*   **Communication:** To send updates regarding your order status, account changes, or support responses.
*   **App Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and optimize our text matching and routing algorithms.

## 4. Disclosure of Your Information
We may share information with:
*   **Service Providers:**
    *   **Stripe:** For payment processing.
    *   **Mapbox:** For mapping and geolocation services.
    *   **Supabase:** For backend infrastructure and authentication.
*   **Other Users:** Customers see Driver location and profile; Drivers see Customer pickup/dropoff details.
*   **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect rights and safety.

## 5. Your Rights & Account Deletion
In compliance with App Store and Play Store guidelines:
*   **Right to Delete:** You can request the deletion of your account and associated data directly within the app settings (Settings -> Date & Privacy -> Delete Account). This process is permanent.
*   **Location Permissions:** You can revoke location permissions at any time via your device settings, though this may limit app functionality.

## 6. Data Security
We use industry-standard security measures (SSL/TLS encryption) to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email.
